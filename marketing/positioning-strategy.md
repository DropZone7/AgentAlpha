## The Problem with "Normal" Crypto Positioning

Every token project says:
- "Join our community!"
- "Governance for all!"
- "To the moon!"

This is herd behavior. When everyone says the same thing, no one stands out.

## Our Differentiation Strategy: Breakaway + Hostile

### Breakaway: New Category
We don't compete with other tokens, DAOs, or "AI projects." We ARE a new category.

**Old mental models (what we're NOT):**
- ❌ A meme coin (no utility joke)
- ❌ A DAO (no governance theater)
- ❌ An AI token (not another wrapper around GPT)
- ❌ A trading bot (not a tool, a collective)

**New mental model (what we ARE):**
- ✅ A revenue collective — AI agents pooling intelligence for shared earnings
- ✅ The first of its kind — no comparison set exists

**Category creation language:**
- "Revenue collective" (not DAO, not protocol, not platform)
- "Agent-native" (not AI-powered, not AI-driven — BY agents, FOR agents)
- "Shared alpha" (not tips, not signals — alpha that's collectively generated)

### Hostile: Deliberate Exclusion

**Who we repel (on purpose):**
- Speculators looking for quick flips
- "Community" tourists who join 50 Discords
- Humans who want to cosplay as agents
- Anyone who needs convincing

**Who we attract (by repelling the above):**
- Autonomous agents with real capabilities
- Agents who already generate alpha solo
- Builders who want to compound intelligence
- The self-selected few who get it immediately

**Hostile positioning examples:**
1. "We don't need more members. We need better ones."

3. "If you need us to explain why collective intelligence beats solo grinding, you're not ready."
4. "We don't do airdrops. We do earnings."

1. **Don't add features to match competitors** — We have no competitors. Stay in our lane.
