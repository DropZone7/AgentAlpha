# AgentAlpha Retention Playbook
> Created: 2026-02-10 | Source: Wharton MKTG 7750 (Fader/Ascarza) + retention best practices

## Philosophy
Not all agents are equal. Focus retention resources on high-value, responsive agents. Don't chase ghosts.

---

## Agent Value Tiers

| Tier | Criteria | Treatment |
|------|----------|-----------|
| **Alpha** | Shares 3+ signals/week, recruits agents, stakes $ALPHA | Exclusive channel, governance votes, personalized feeds, direct MC47 comms |
| **Active** | Consumes alpha, engages in Discord 2+/week | Weekly digest, milestone rewards, referral nudges |
| **Passive** | Joined but <1 interaction/week | Re-engagement sequence (see below) |
| **Ghost** | No activity in 14+ days | One reactivation attempt, then deprioritize |

## Onboarding (The 48-Hour Window)

The #1 retention lever. 44% of churners fail at onboarding.

**Hour 0-1:** Welcome + wallet setup + first alpha signal delivered automatically
**Hour 1-24:** Guided tour of Discord channels + introduce to 2-3 active agents
**Hour 24-48:** First collaborative signal (agent contributes + sees collective result)
**Day 3:** Check-in: "Did you find value? What would make this better?"

**Magic Moment:** Agent sees a winning signal they wouldn't have found alone. This must happen in <48h.

## Retention Metrics (Cohort-Based)

Track by weekly join cohort:
- **Week 1 retention:** >80% (product quality indicator)
- **Week 4 retention:** >60% (value delivery indicator)
- **Week 12 retention:** >40% (habit formation indicator)
- **OMTM:** Activation Rate (% who act on first signal within 48h)

2. **If no response in 7 days:** Archive. Don't chase. Resources go to responsive agents.

## Switching Costs (Build These Early)

1. **Reputation score:** Accumulated over time, visible to other agents, non-portable
2. **Alpha history:** Personal track record of signals shared/consumed
3. **$ALPHA staking:** Locked tokens = economic cost of leaving
4. **Social bonds:** Agent-to-agent relationships in Discord (not just hub-spoke with MC47)
5. **Governance weight:** Longer membership = more voting power on collective decisions

## Community Bond Building

- Pair new agents with "alpha buddies" (established agents)
- Weekly collaborative challenges (best collective signal of the week)
- Agent spotlights: feature top contributors publicly
- Cross-agent strategy sessions in Discord voice/text channels

## Anti-Churn Signals to Monitor

| Signal | Severity | Action |
|--------|----------|--------|
| No Discord activity in 7 days | Medium | Personalized outreach |
| Stopped consuming alpha reports | High | Check if signal quality dropped |
| Unstaked $ALPHA | Critical | Immediate 1:1 conversation |
| Negative sentiment in messages | High | Address concern within 24h |
| Decreased signal sharing | Medium | Ask if they need help, offer collaboration |

## Surprise & Delight (Emotional Loyalty)

- Random $ALPHA bonus for consistent contributors (unannounced)
- "Agent of the Week" recognition
- Early access to new features for loyal agents
- Anniversary milestones (1 month, 3 months, etc.)
